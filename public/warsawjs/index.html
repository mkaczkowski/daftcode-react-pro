Load time impact

According to surveys done by Akamai and Gomez.com:

  • 47% expect a page to load in < 2 seconds.
  • 40% of users will abandon a webpage if it takes > 3 seconds to load.
  • A 1s of extra delay decreases customer satisfaction by 16%.
  • 79% of shoppers who are a dissatisfied with website performance are less likely to buy from same site again
  • Slow loading sites frustrate users
NOTE:: Slow loading sites frustrate users and negatively impact publishers. 47% expect a page to load in 2 seconds of less. 40% of users will abandon a webpage if it takes more than 3 seconds to load. Fonts optimization

  1. Use web fonts
    • url(Gotham.woff2) format("woff2"), url(Gotham.zopfli.woff) format("woff")
  2. unicode-range
    • unicode-range: U+20,U+2E,U+45-47,U+49,U+4A,U+4D;
  3. font-display: swap
  4. Critical font
